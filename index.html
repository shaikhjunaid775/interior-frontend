Who We are

About the Consortium Interior
Solutions

Navin Kotian is a highly accomplished individual and the proud owner of Consortium Interior Solutions, a reputable interior design company based in Mumbai, India. With an unwavering passion for design and an exceptional eye for detail, Navin has led his company to great heights since its establishment in 2008.

With an impressive track record, Navin Kotian has successfully completed over 50 projects during his career, showcasing his talent and expertise in the field of interior design. His dedication to delivering high-quality work and creating aesthetically pleasing spaces has earned him widespread recognition throughout Mumbai and beyond.

Residential Interior

Residential interior design focuses on creating functional and aesthetically pleasing living spaces by carefully selecting furniture, colors, and materials to enhance comfort and reflect the homeowner's style and preferences.

Modular Kitchen

A modular kitchen is a pre-designed and pre-fabricated kitchen layout that consists of standardized units and cabinets. It offers efficient storage solutions, flexibility in design, and easy installation, making it a popular choice for modern homes.
